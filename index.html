About Wyatt Archer

I’ve been making music in one form or another for more than 25 years. It started in church, then grew into different side projects over the years — writing, playing, experimenting, chasing ideas whenever they showed up.

With the way technology has evolved, I decided to build something new: an online persona and digital artist named Wyatt Archer — named after my dog. What started as curiosity has turned into something I genuinely love. Using modern tools, apps, plugins, and instruments, I can take a concept, build the music, write the lyrics, record my guitar, and bring a song to life from start to finish.

Some of the songs reflect my own journey — the highs, the mistakes, the lessons. Others are stories pulled from imagination or inspired by people and places along the way. But all of them come from somewhere honest.

If you enjoy what you hear, I’d be grateful for a follow and a like to stay connected as new music rolls out.
